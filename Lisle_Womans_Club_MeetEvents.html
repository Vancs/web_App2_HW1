Meetings and Events

Each of our meetings begins with general business to keep members up-to-date and informed of club information and activities. We also arrange an interesting program on topics that inspire, entertain or motivate. Social time and light refreshments are all part of the evening.

Meetings

Meetings every first wednesday each month from September through June, 7:00pm to 9:00pm. Meeting room at the Lisle Police Station, 5040 Lincoln Ave. (corner of Short St., and Rt. 53), Lisle, IL. Dues are $40.00 Annually. This will provide you the club directory and newsletters, publications from GFWC, and support our meeting programs.

Upcoming Events
