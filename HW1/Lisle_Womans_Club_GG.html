Garden Gait

Date: June 14,2020

Garden Gait is our premier fundraising activity, showcasing delightful homeowner gardens in Lisle. Starting at Lisle Depot station, have refreshments and visit our craft fair vendors. This event supports our scholarship program.

Click here to see a video about our Garden Gait event.
